# Creator fees overview

Updated 1 month ago

Whether you're new to Patreon or a long-time creator, this guide breaks down all the fees you can expect when offering paid memberships or selling digital products.

If you published your creator page after August 4, 2025, please note that you are on our standard 10% pricing plan. Creators who published their page on or before August 4, 2025, will not see any increase. If you unpublish your page or if Patreon unpublishes your page for any reason, you’ll be subject to Patreon’s new, standard 10% plan + applicable taxes and payment processing fees.

Get the details about this change and what to expect as an existing or new creator by visiting our guide on our standard pricing plan.

### Platform fees overview

The platform fee covers the use of Patreon’s platform and is a percentage of successfully processed membership and one-time purchases. We calculate your platform fee based on the payment amount, excluding sales tax.

How to check which plan you're on

    Visit your Account Settings page: https://www.patreon.com/settings-creator/account 
    Your current pricing plan will be listed at the top of your settings under the “monetization” header

If you're unsure, please feel free to submit a request with our Product Support team for help.

### Standard 10% Platform Plan (Effective after August 4, 2025)

Who does this plan apply to?

The standard 10% pricing plan + relevant taxes applies to any creator who publishes their Patreon page after August 4, 2025. If you're on a legacy plan and unpublish your page (or if Patreon does) for any reason, you'll move to the standard plan when you republish. The same applies if you're on the Founders plan and change your currency—you’ll need to agree to the standard pricing before making that change.

What triggers a switch to the standard pricing plan?

If any of the following happens after August 4, 2025, you’ll be moved to the standard 10% platform plan:

    You unpublish your page (even briefly)
    Patreon unpublishes your page for any reason
    You republish your page after August 4, 2025
    You’re on the Founders plan and change your currency (this requires agreeing to the standard 10% plan)

What’s included in the 10% platform fee?

The standard plan offers all the core features of Patreon. The 10% platform fee gives you access to all of Patreon’s core features, including:

    Hosted creator page
    Monthly and annual memberships
    Digital product sales (one-time purchases)
    Video hosting (up to 100 hours/month if you have paying fans)
    Community tools: chats, polls, comments
    Audience and growth insights

### Payment fees for legacy platform plans (For pages published on or before August 4, 2025)

The following payment processing rates are only applicable to creators who published their page on or before August 4, 2025. If you unpublish your creator page for any amount of time, or if Patreon unpublishes your page for any reason, your account will be subject to Patreon’s standardized platform plan + applicable taxes and standard payment processing rate when you republish your page.

What factors into your total payment fees?

You can confirm your platform plan in your Settings. Your payment fees apply on a per-payment basis and include several components, depending on how your page is set up and where your fans are located.

The following factors contribute to your total payment fee:

     
        Your tier price
        If you're on a legacy platform plan, your tier price determines whether the standard or micropayment processing rate applies
        Your payout currency
        Your payout currency determines which payment rate applies. For creators on legacy plans, it also affects whether the micropayment rate is used
        Additionally, the amount paid out is impacted by the following:
            Sales tax or VAT (if required)
            In some regions, tax must be collected from your members by law. Your payment processing fee is calculated based on the total payment amount, including any applicable tax. Learn more about sales tax and VAT
            Currency conversion (if applicable)
            A 2.5% currency conversion fee applies when a fan pays in a currency that’s different from your payout currency. This fee is calculated on the full processed amount, including tax

### Payment processing rates for the Founders plan (For pages published on or before May 7, 2019)

You’re on the Founders plan if you joined Patreon before May 7, 2019, and haven’t upgraded your plan in the past, changed your currency, or had your creator page unpublished by you or by Patreon for any reason.

The following factors contribute to your total payment fees:

    Your tier price
    If you're on a legacy platform plan, your tier price determines whether the standard or micropayment processing rate applies
    Shared membership
    Fees are split between creators on our Founders plan when multiple memberships are combined in one transaction
    A member’s payment method
    Different processing rates are applied based on your members’ payment method
    Additionally, the amount paid out is impacted by the following:
        Sales tax or VAT (if required)
        In some regions, tax must be collected from your members by law. Your payment processing fee is calculated based on the total payment amount, including any applicable tax. Learn more about sales tax and VAT
        Currency conversion (if applicable)
        A 2.5% currency conversion fee applies when a fan pays in a currency that’s different from your payout currency. This fee is calculated on the full processed amount, including tax

Founders can only receive payouts in USD. If you are on our Founders plan and change your currency, your creator page will be subject to our updated 10% + standard process fees.

How shared membership affects fees

If a member supports multiple creators in a single transaction, and you’re on the Founders plan, you’ll share the processing fee with those other creators. This may reduce your individual fee for that transaction.


### Creator-to-creator membership payments

When a creator’s Patreon balance is used to cover a membership payment to another creator, Patreon’s payment processing fee is not applied. If a creator’s Patreon balance doesn’t cover the full amount of their membership payment, we’ll charge their payment method, and payment processing fees will apply.


### Fees on one-time purchases

It’s free to list your work for sale on Patreon (e.g., digital products, posts, and collections). One-time purchases are subject to the same fees as membership payments:

    Platform fee
    Between 5% and 12% of successfully processed sales + applicable taxes, depending on your platform plan
    Payment processing fee
    Varies by currency (e.g., 2.9% + $0.30 USD) standard payment process rate

Creators who started selling digital products before May 22, 2025, may be on a 5% one-time purchase fee + applicable taxes and payment processing rates.

Learn more about how sales work in our selling one-time purchases guide.


### Currency conversion fees

A 2.5% currency conversion fee applies to any payment made in a currency different from your payout currency.

This fee is:

    Calculated on the full payment (including tax)
    Refunded if the member is refunded
    Shown under the Payment Fees column in your Earnings dashboard

You will receive your portion of membership payments in your payout currency based on the average exchange rate from the day before the payment was successfully processed.

Where supported and as applicable, we automatically convert the prices of offerings and memberships into a member’s local currency based on a currency conversion rate that may be determined at our sole discretion. You can learn more about how tiers are converted into other currencies.

If a paid member pays you in your payout currency, then there is no currency conversion fee applied.


### Taxes applied to creator fees

Where required by law, Patreon collects VAT, GST, or sales tax on platform fees. These taxes:

    Are based on your business location and local regulations
    Are shown as a separate line item

Visit our Taxes on creator fees article to learn more about where Patreon applies taxes on fees and how it may affect you.

Funds in your account balance already reflect the deduction of your platform fee, payment processing fees, and currency conversion fees. Patreon deducts a payout transaction fee when your balance is transferred from your Patreon account.

Your payout fee is not listed in the breakdown of fees within your creator earnings dashboard; however, you may find the payout fee for your payout method and currency of choice in the tables below.

### iOS In-App purchase fees

For eligible purchases made in the Patreon iOS app, Apple’s in-app purchase system applies a 30% App Store fee. Patreon does not charge additional payment processing fees on iOS transactions, but the platform fee still applies.

    One-time purchases
    In January 2024, we began gradually rolling out iOS in-app purchases for Commerce. Learn more by visiting the iOS in-app purchases for Commerce
    Memberships
    In November 2024, we began rolling out Apple’s in-app purchase system for new memberships. Learn more by visiting how iOS in-app purchases work on Patreon
    U.S. customers
    Fans in the U.S. may have the option to complete purchases through Patreon’s mobile web checkout instead of using Apple’s in-app purchase flow. Purchases completed on the mobile web are not subject to Apple’s App Store fee
